Elon Musk

Electric Cars, Renewable Energy, and A Mission to Mars

Musk's three companies SpaceX, Tesla, and SolarCity are helping pave the way for a more sustainable future.

Here's a time line of Elon Musk's life:

  • 1971 - Born in South Africa
  • 1981 - Teaches himself computer programming at age 10.
  • 1983 - Sells a computer game he created for $500 to a PC magazine.
  • 1989 - Moves to Canada.
  • 1994 - Graduates from the University of Pennsylvannia with a degree in Physics.
  • 1995 - Alongside his brother, creates Zip2, a web software company. Recieves $22 million for the sale of the company
  • 1999 - Creates the company X.com and eventually merges with PayPal. PayPal is acquired two years later by eBay for $1.2 billion
  • 2001 - Creates the space exploration company SpaceX.
  • 2006 - Helps co-found SolarCity, the second largest provider of solar panels in the United States.
  • 2008 - SpaceX's Falcon 1 rocket becomes the first privately funded liquid-fueled vehicle to put a satellite into Earths orbit.
  • 2008 - Assumes leadership of Tesla Motors as the CEO and product architect.
  • 2013 - Unveils a high-speed transportation system called the Hyperloop.
  • 2015 - Announces the creation of OpenAI, a not-for-profit Artificial Intelligence company.
